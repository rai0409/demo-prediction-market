# Legal And Safety Notes

This repository is a technical MVP for a local demo prediction market viewer.

It is not:

- legal advice
- investment advice
- betting advice
- trading advice
- a production service

## Demo Points

The app displays reference market data and allows local-only simulation with free demo points. Demo points have no monetary value.

Demo points cannot be purchased, withdrawn, cashed out, transferred, converted to money, converted to crypto, exchanged for external points, or exchanged for gifts or prizes.

## Not Implemented

The app does not perform real trading, does not perform real betting, does not place Polymarket orders, does not connect wallets, does not use private credentials, and does not support deposits, withdrawals, cashout, paid point purchase, or external point exchange.

## Before Any Production Use

Operators should review applicable laws, platform terms, consumer protection obligations, financial regulations, gambling regulations, data retention requirements, and regional restrictions before adapting any prediction market experience for production use.

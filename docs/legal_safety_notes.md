# Legal And Safety Notes

This repository is a technical MVP for a local demo prediction market viewer. It is not legal advice, investment advice, betting advice, or trading advice.

The app displays reference market data and allows local-only simulation with free demo points. Demo points have no monetary value.

The app does not perform real trading, does not place real orders, does not connect wallets, and does not support deposits, withdrawals, cashout, paid point purchase, or external point exchange.

Operators should review applicable laws and platform terms before adapting any prediction market experience for production use.
